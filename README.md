**Cognize** is a mindfulness-focused iOS app designed to help users become more intentional with their phone usage. Rather than just blocking apps or reducing screen time, Cognize empowers users to reflect on their goals, log their intentions, and develop a conscious relationship with technology.

---

## Project Status

**Cognize is currently in early development.**  
Features are actively being prototyped, and UX concepts are still evolving.

---

## Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

Unlike traditional screen time or productivity apps, Cognize:

- Helps users **realize their intention** before using their phone
- Builds **consciousness**, not just control
- Introduces a unique **category-based restriction system**
- Encourages **reflection**, not guilt
